how to name things in the code:
-------------------------------
- `RT_` stands for "render task" (used only for functions related to rendering tasks).
- `RV_` stands for "render variables" (used for variables related to rendering).
- `G_` stands for "global" (used for global variables or functions).
- `M_` stands for "movement" (used for movement-related logic or variables).
- `L_` stands for "level" (used for level-related logic or variables).
- `C_` stands for "console" (used for console-related logic or variables).
-------------------------------
[NOTE] not all parts of the engine fully adhere to these naming conventions yet.
